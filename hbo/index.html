HBO Max

What is HBO Max? Is it like HBO or HBO Go? HBO Max is all of them combined! HBO Max launched on May 27, 2020, with new and classic content. The streaming service has 10,000 hours of programming such as series, movies, and specials. HBO Max can be accessed by being subscribed to their service, having a free trial, or your cable provider.

How to Use HBO Max

HBO Max like any other streaming service has a monthly fee, the fee starts at 14.99. HBO Max has a free trial option that lasts for 7 days. When trying to access HBO Max you can check to see if your cable has a package or your phone company. There are a variety of tv providers that offer HBO Max within your plan, for example, AT&T U-verse, Xfinity, DIRECTV, and more. Having an AT&T phone service can let you sign in from your AT&T account to access HBO Max. Some colleges also provide a subscription to HBO as long as you sign in with your school such as CSUMB.
